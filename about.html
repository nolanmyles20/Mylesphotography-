About Us

Myles Photography LLC was founded with a passion for aerial perspective and visual storytelling. Based in Oscoda, Michigan, we provide high-quality drone and real estate photography that helps properties stand out in today’s competitive market.

Every image we capture is guided by precision, artistry, and a deep understanding of how light, angles, and elevation create emotion.

FAA Certified

Myles Photography LLC proudly operates as an FAA-Certified Drone Pilot under Part 107, ensuring safe and compliant aerial operations for every shoot. All flights are conducted within FAA-approved guidelines for altitude, airspace, and safety.

Our Mission

To provide every client with breathtaking, professional-grade aerial photography that enhances their visual marketing and tells their story from a new perspective.
